About

Hi, my name is Michael, people call me Mike or Mikey.

I am a data-engineer with big interest in public data and predictive analytics. Recent projects have led me to data mining a range of subjects. From the metro transit authority, to every major underwear retailer in the USA for all kinds of insights. I could easily see a dream job in taking all sorts of freely given data from users. Then organizing, storing, transforming, and visualizing all of it back to them, for them.

Taking on an M.S. in Data Science from a backgrond in Liberal Arts was no easy task for me, but I could not have asked for a better start. Recommender systems, simulations, and queuing theory worked harmonously alongside computational mathematics, statistics and probabilites.

My hobbies include social events, anything in the sun, any type of game, and model building (Figurines or data-based).
